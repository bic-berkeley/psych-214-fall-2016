Classes¶

Classes are on Monday 13.30 – 16.30pm.

We will usually break this up into two sessions, with a 15 minute break in-between.

  • August 29: introduction, Python, images
  • September 12: arrays, images and plotting
  • September 19: version control, 4D arrays, diagnostics
  • September 26: vectors, projection and PCA
  • October 3: statistics on brain images
  • October 10: exploring the general linear model
  • October 17: project pitch, ANOVA with the GLM
  • October 24: the HRF, inference on statistical maps
  • October 31: slice timing and motion correction
  • November 7: affine and cross-modality registration
  • November 14: cross-subject registration
  • November 21: capstone; scripting an analysis
  • November 28: TBD

Monday September 5th is an academic holiday.

Final project presentations during class hours, Monday 5 December (RRR week).

Final project submissions by midnight Friday 17th December.
