Classes¶

Classes are on Monday 13.30 – 16.30pm.

We will usually break this up into two sessions, with a 15 minute break in-between.

  • August 29: introduction, Python, images
  • September 19: 2D / 3D arrays and plotting
  • September 19: version control, 4D arrays
  • September 26: diagnostics and testing
  • October 3: simple statistics on brain images
  • October 10: modeling the hemodynamic response function
  • October 17: regression and the general linear model
  • October 24: t and F contrasts, statistical inference
  • October 31: choosing a statistical model
  • November 7: slice-timing / 1D interpolation
  • November 14: optimization, 2D interpolation
  • November 21: coordinate systems; cross-modality registration
  • November 28: cross-subject registration

Monday September 5th is an academic holiday.

Final project presentations during class hours, Monday 5 December (RRR week).

Final project submissions by midnight Friday 17th December.
