Classes¶

Classes are on Monday 13.30 – 16.30pm.

We will usually break this up into two sessions, with a 15 minute break in-between.

  • Introduction, Python, images
  • Arrays, images and plotting
  • Version control, 4D arrays, diagnostics
  • Vectors, projection and PCA
  • Statistics on brain images
  • Exploring the general linear model
  • Project pitch, ANOVA with the GLM
  • The HRF, modeling and statistical maps
  • Multiple comparison correction
  • Slice timing and motion correction
  • Affine and cross-modality registration
  • Cross-subject registration
  • Exploring cross-subject registration

Monday September 5th is an academic holiday.

Final project presentations during class hours, Monday 5 December (RRR week).

Final project submissions by midnight Friday 17th December.
